# Privacy Policy for GymIQ

**Last updated:** May 2026

## What Data We Collect

GymIQ collects the following data that gym owners enter:
- **Member information**: names, phone numbers, weight, dates of birth, profile photos
- **Payment records**: payment amounts, dates, plan names
- **Gym profile**: gym name, owner details, logo

## Where Data Is Stored

- **On your device**: A local SQLite database stores all data for offline access.
- **Cloud (when subscribed)**: If you have an active GymIQ subscription, data is synced to **Google Firebase Firestore**, a cloud database service hosted by Google LLC. This enables multi-device access and automatic backups.

## Third-Party Services

GymIQ uses the following third-party services:
- **Google Firebase** (Authentication, Firestore database, Storage) — [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **WhatsApp** (via deep links for sending reminders) — messages are composed by the app but sent by the WhatsApp app on your device
- **Google Play** (in-app purchases for subscription) — [Google Play Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell your data. Member data is only accessible to the gym owner and staff accounts authorized by the gym owner. Firebase's access is governed by security rules that restrict data to authorized users only.

## Data Deletion

To delete your gym's data, go to **Settings → Data Management → Delete All Data** in the app, or contact us. Deleting the app removes the local database. Cloud data can be deleted on request.

## Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact the app developer through the Google Play Store listing.
